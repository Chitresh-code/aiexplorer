# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Quick answers to common questions about AIExplorer.

## Account & Access

### Q: How do I get access to AIExplorer?
**A:** Contact your system administrator or IT department to request access. You'll need an active corporate email account. Access is typically granted within 1-2 business days.

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
**A:** Click **"Sign In"** → **"Forgot Password"** and follow the reset instructions sent to your email. If you're using Azure AD/Microsoft 365, use your organization's standard password reset process.

### Q: Can I access AIExplorer from home?
**A:** Yes, AIExplorer is accessible from anywhere with internet access. However, your organization may have network policies or VPN requirements. Check with IT.

### Q: What browsers are supported?
**A:** AIExplorer works on:
- Chrome (latest 2 versions)
- Firefox (latest 2 versions)
- Safari (latest 2 versions)
- Edge (latest 2 versions)

Use the latest version for best experience.

## Use Cases & Submissions

### Q: What makes a good AI use case?
**A:** Good use cases have:
- Clear business problem they solve
- Measurable success metrics
- Available or obtainable data
- Realistic resource requirements
- Executive sponsorship
- Strong business value proposition

See [Best Practices](./submitting-usecases.md#best-practices) for details.

### Q: Can I save my submission as a draft?
**A:** Yes! Your form auto-saves every 2 minutes. You can leave and return later. Find it in **Submit** → **My Submissions** → **Drafts**.

### Q: How long does the submission process take?
**A:** The full review process typically takes 3-6 weeks:
- Admin review: 1-2 weeks
- Stakeholder review: 1-2 weeks
- Technical review: 1-2 weeks
- Final decision: 1 week

### Q: Can I edit my submission after submitting?
**A:** Once submitted, you cannot edit directly. If changes are requested, you can revise and resubmit. If approved, contact your project owner to update details.

### Q: What happens if my use case is rejected?
**A:** You'll receive feedback explaining why. Common reasons:
- Insufficient data availability
- Unclear business value
- Resource constraints
- Similar existing solution
- Strategic misalignment

You can address feedback and resubmit.

### Q: Can multiple people collaborate on a use case submission?
**A:** The primary submitter must complete the form, but you can:
- Add stakeholders during submission
- Request colleague input before submitting
- Resubmit with additional information

### Q: Is there a use case template?
**A:** Yes! Go to **Submit** → **Templates** to download example use cases. These show expected format and level of detail.

## Projects & Management

### Q: How do I join an existing project?
**A:** 
1. Ask your manager or project owner
2. They add you as team member
3. Project appears on your dashboard
4. You'll receive notification

### Q: Can I have multiple roles on a project?
**A:** No, each person has one primary role. However, you can have different roles on different projects.

### Q: What if the project timeline changes?
**A:** Project owner updates the timeline in project settings. All team members are notified of changes. Milestones auto-adjust if specified as relative dates.

### Q: How often should I update project status?
**A:** Status updates are typically:
- Weekly: Quick team sync
- Monthly: Detailed executive update
- Per milestone: Completion notification
- As-needed: For critical issues or changes

### Q: Can I export project data?
**A:** Yes! Click **"Export"** on any project to download as:
- PDF (formatted report)
- Excel (data for analysis)
- CSV (import to other tools)

## Metrics & Reporting

### Q: How are success metrics calculated?
**A:** Metrics defined during project setup are tracked:
- Collected from project activities
- Calculated automatically where possible
- Manually updated when needed
- Compared against targets

### Q: Can I create custom reports?
**A:** Yes, in **Reports** section:
1. Click **"Custom Report"**
2. Select metrics to include
3. Choose time period
4. Set filters
5. Generate and download

### Q: How often are reports updated?
**A:** Reports update:
- Real-time for dashboard metrics
- Daily for detailed analytics
- Weekly for executive summaries
- On-demand for custom reports

### Q: Can I share reports with others?
**A:** Yes, reports have sharing options:
- Generate shareable link
- Export as PDF
- Send via email
- Embed in presentations

## Notifications & Communication

### Q: How do I control notifications?
**A:** In **Settings** → **Notifications**:
- Choose notification types
- Set email frequency (immediately, daily digest, weekly)
- Disable specific alerts
- Choose mobile notifications

### Q: I'm getting too many notifications
**A:** Options to reduce notifications:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Disable non-essential alerts
3. Choose digest format instead of immediate
4. Unsubscribe from specific projects

### Q: Can I mention specific people?
**A:** Yes! In comments, type `@` followed by their name. They'll be notified and can respond.

### Q: Are notifications real-time?
**A:** Email notifications may have 5-15 minute delay. In-app notifications appear immediately. SMS alerts (if enabled) are also near real-time.

## Technical Issues

### Q: The page won't load. What should I do?
**A:**
1. Try refreshing with `Ctrl+Shift+R`
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check internet connection
5. Contact IT if persists

### Q: I can't find a project I was just viewing
**A:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Check filters (may be hiding it)
3. Use search bar
4. Verify you still have access
5. Contact administrator

### Q: Documents won't upload. Why?
**A:** Check:
- File size less than 10MB
- File format supported (PDF, Word, Excel, images)
- Internet connection stable
- Try different file if corrupted

### Q: Is AIExplorer mobile-friendly?
**A:** Yes! Full functionality on:
- Smartphones (iOS and Android)
- Tablets
- Any device with modern browser

Mobile interface auto-optimizes layout.

## Data & Privacy

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes, AIExplorer uses:
- Enterprise-grade encryption
- Microsoft Azure infrastructure
- Azure AD authentication
- Regular security audits
- Compliance with organization policies

### Q: How long is my data retained?
**A:** Standard retention:
- Active data: Indefinite while relevant
- Completed projects: 7 years
- Deleted items: 30-day recovery period
- Contact IT for specific requirements

### Q: Can I download my data?
**A:** Yes! You can export:
- Individual project data
- Custom reports
- All your submissions
- Activity history (limited)

Contact IT for bulk data exports.

### Q: Who can see my information?
**A:** Access controlled by:
- Your role (user, admin, etc.)
- Project participation
- Department
- Explicit sharing permissions
- Organization policies

Privacy settings in **Settings** → **Privacy**.

### Q: How do I delete my account?
**A:** Contact your organization's IT department or system administrator. Note:
- Existing project data may be retained
- Cannot delete if you're only project owner
- Manager approval may be required

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Q: I see "Permission Denied" error
**A:**
1. Verify you have access to that project
2. Contact project owner
3. Ask administrator to grant access
4. May need role change

### Q: My changes aren't saving
**A:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Try again (may be temporary issue)
3. Check browser console for errors
4. Try different browser
5. Clear cache

### Q: I need to recover a deleted item
**A:** Recently deleted items are recoverable:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Trash**
2. Find the item
3. Click **"Restore"**
4. Note: 30-day recovery window

### Q: Search returns no results
**A:**
1. Check spelling
2. Try partial search
3. Use filters to narrow results
4. Search may be limited by permissions
