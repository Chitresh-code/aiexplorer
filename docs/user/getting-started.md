## Prerequisites

Before you can use AIExplorer, you need:

- An active corporate email account
- Access to the internet and modern web browser
- Login credentials provided by your organization
- Permission from your organization to use AIExplorer

Contact your IT department or system administrator if you don't have access.

## Accessing AIExplorer

### Step 1: Open AIExplorer

Navigate to your organization's AIExplorer instance:

```
https://aiexplorer.yourcompany.com
```

### Step 2: Sign In

Click the **"Sign In"** button in the top-right corner.

### Step 3: Authenticate

You'll be directed to your organization's login page (Azure AD/Microsoft 365):

1. Enter your corporate email address
2. Enter your password
3. Complete any additional authentication (MFA if required)

You'll be redirected back to AIExplorer.

## First Login

### What You'll See

On your first login, you'll see:

- Welcome banner with quick start options
- Dashboard overview
- Navigation sidebar on the left
- Your profile information in the top-right

### Tour the Interface

Take time to explore:

- **Navigation Sidebar** (left) - Access main sections
- **Dashboard** (center) - Overview of AI initiatives
- **User Menu** (top-right) - Profile and settings
- **Search Bar** (top) - Quick search functionality

## Profile Setup

### Complete Your Profile

1. Click your **name/avatar** in the top-right corner
2. Select **"Profile Settings"**
3. Update the following:
   - **Photo**: Add a profile picture
   - **Title**: Your job title
   - **Department**: Your department name
   - **Expertise**: Areas of AI interest
   - **Contact Info**: Phone and secondary email

### Set Preferences

In **"Settings"** → **"Preferences"**:

- **Notifications**: Choose what notifications you receive
- **Email Frequency**: Digest preferences
- **Language**: Your preferred language
- **Theme**: Light or dark mode

## Troubleshooting First Login

### Can't Sign In

1. **Verify credentials**: Check your email and password
2. **Try incognito mode**: Clear browser cache issues
3. **Check browser**: Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
4. **Contact support**: If problems persist

### Page Won't Load

1. Check your internet connection
2. Disable browser extensions temporarily
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser

### Can't See All Features

- Ensure your account has been fully activated
- Check with your administrator about permissions
- Refresh the page with **Ctrl+Shift+R**
