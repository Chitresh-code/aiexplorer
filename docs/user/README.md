## What is AIExplorer?

AIExplorer is a comprehensive platform designed to help your organization:

- **Submit and Track** AI use cases from concept to deployment
- **Collaborate** with stakeholders and team members
- **Monitor Progress** through different implementation phases
- **Measure Success** with detailed metrics and reporting
- **Build a Gallery** of AI initiatives across the enterprise

## Key Concepts

### Use Case
A description of how AI can solve a specific business problem or opportunity within your organization.

### Project
A tracked initiative to implement an AI use case with defined timeline, resources, and success metrics.

### Phase
Different stages of implementation: Discovery → Development → Testing → Deployment → Monitoring

### Stakeholder
A person involved in or affected by an AI use case (sponsor, developer, business owner, etc.)
